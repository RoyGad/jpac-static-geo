Use of this Index

This index has been prepared to assist the accurate assessment of the geographical risks and contains information on:

  • Malaria
  • T. cruzi
  • HIV risk in Sub Saharan Africa
  • WNV
  • Chikungunya virus (CHIKV)
  • Viral Haemorrhagic Fever Risk (endemic countries only)
